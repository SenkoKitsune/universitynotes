Lagrange multipliers
	Find a max/min of a function $(fx,y)$ subject to a constraint $g(x,y)$ = constant
		$f_x = \lambda g(x)$
		$f_y = \lambda g(y)$
		$\lambda = k$ a constant
	Also works for $f(x,y,z)$ subject to $g(x,y,z) = k$
		$f_x = \lambda g(x)$
		$f_y = \lambda g(y)$
		$f_z = \lambda g(z)$
		$\lambda = k$ a constant
